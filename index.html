ImageCrop

Crop images with pixel-perfect precision.

ImageCrop is a simple desktop tool for creators, teams, and anyone who needs precise, consistent image cropping. Crop from center or set custom margins, then batch-process hundreds of images with confidence. Part of the ImageUtils toolkit.

Two crop modes

Center crop: Keep the center of your image and discard edges evenly. Margin crop: Remove specific pixels from each side (top, right, bottom, left).

Batch processing

Process hundreds of images at once with the same crop settings. Preserves folder structure and image quality.

For everyone

Designers, e-commerce teams, photographers, and content creators can crop product photos, social media assets, and thumbnails to exact dimensions.
